about me

Hello there! Welcome to my website. Just in case you don't know who I am, my name is Soo Park, and I'm a software/electrical engineer located in Boulder, Colorado. My focus and interests are mostly in the software development realm specializing in user interface development.


I was born and raised in Seoul, S.Korea and hailed to the beautiful state of Colorado in middle school. I graduated from University of Colorado, Boulder with a B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering degree with a focus in software and semiconductor technology as well as a minor in Technology, Arts and Media. I'm currently back in school to complete a minor in Computer Science.


Aside from being a programmer, I'm also an avid traveler. You can find pictures that I've taken from all over the world. I have marked all countries that I've visited so far and I hope it grows over the years!


On this website, you will find my professional as well as personal work/portfolio ranging from art work to graphic design, and software development.
